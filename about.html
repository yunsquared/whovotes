About Our Team

We're a team of information science and government students at Cornell University who first met in Professor Aditya Vashistha's Computing & Global Development course in the fall of 2020. The class focus lies at the intersection of ICT& Development (ICTD), Human-Computer Interaction, and Accessibility. We were inspired and challenged to think about socially impactful solutions addressing issues for underserved communiites, and with the impending presidential election months away, Broadband Votes was born. We explore information accessibility issues in an increasingly digital world, and look to better understand how broadband penetration impacts information accessibility and voter turnout in the 21st century.
